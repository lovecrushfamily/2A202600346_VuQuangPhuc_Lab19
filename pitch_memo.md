PITCH MEMO — AI Career Advisor Assistant

## 1. THE PROBLEM
Career advisors, recruiters, and university career centers still spend hours every week opening multiple job platforms, copying data into sheets, and manually counting skill and salary trends. By the time the analysis is done, the market has already moved, so the advice they give students or hiring managers is often late, shallow, or hard to defend.

## 2. THE INSIGHT
The real gap is not “another AI chatbot”; it is a trusted data pipeline, because career advisors only use AI when every number has a source, a refresh date, and a clear boundary between aggregate insight and personal advice.

## 3. THE SOLUTION
AI Career Advisor Assistant automatically aggregates public job-market data and turns it into a weekly refreshed dashboard plus chat experience for career advisors. Unlike ChatGPT, it does not answer from generic model memory; it answers from a transparent, aggregate-only workflow with cited sources and visible update timing. AI helps normalize fragmented job data, surface niche trends quickly, and let advisors get usable insight in 1-5 minutes instead of doing manual spreadsheet work.

## 4. WHY NOW
Job-market demand now shifts week by week, so static reports and uncited LLM answers become outdated too fast to support real advisory work. At the same time, AI inference costs, modern frontend/edge tooling, and public-data pipelines are now cheap and mature enough to make weekly refreshed, auditable market insight viable for a focused vertical product.

## 5. TRACTION / PROOF
We are still early, so our strongest proof today is product clarity plus measurable economics. The MVP is already narrowed to 3 core flows: cited market dashboard, insight chat, and onboarding walkthrough; the current product promise is a 7-day refresh cycle, 100% cited outputs, coverage across 4+ job platforms, and 1-5 minute time-to-insight. Our Day 17 aha metric is when a user sees a source-cited dashboard immediately and stays engaged with the product for roughly 2 hours per week. In our Day 18 base case, unit economics are healthy: ARPU 600,000 VND/month, gross margin 60%, LTV/CAC 4.8x, CAC payback 4.2 months, and project payback 15 months.

## 6. THE ASK
We are raising 500,000,000 VND pre-seed and looking for 2-3 pilot partners in universities, career centers, or HR teams. Over the next 12 months, this capital will fund productionizing the weekly data pipeline, shipping export/reporting and observability features, and converting the first cohort of paying advisor and research users.
